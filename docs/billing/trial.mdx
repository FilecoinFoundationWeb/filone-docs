## Free trial

Every new account starts with a 30-day free trial. No credit card is required.

| Included | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Storage | 1 TB (1,024 GB) |
| Egress bandwidth | 2 TB |
| API operations | Unlimited |
| Duration | 30 days from account creation |

The trial gives you enough capacity to test real workloads, not just sample uploads. All features are available during the trial.

## What happens when the trial ends

| Phase | Days | What you can do |
|-------|------|-----------------|
| **Active trial** | 0-30 | Full access. Upload, download, manage data. |
| **Grace period** | 31-37 | **Read-only.** Download your data and upgrade to a paid plan. No new uploads. |
| **Expired** | 38+ | Account closed. All data permanently deleted. API keys disabled. |

You will receive email reminders at:
- **Day 15** -- Halfway through your trial, with an upgrade link.
- **Day 25** -- 5 days left, with an upgrade link.
- **Day 31** -- Trial ended, grace period started.
- **Day 36** -- Data deletion in 48 hours.
- **Day 38** -- Deletion confirmed.

:::note
You can upgrade to a paid plan at any time during the trial or grace period. Upgrading restores full access immediately.
:::

## Paid plan pricing

| Component | Price | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Storage | $4.99 / TB / month | Billed based on average daily usage. |
| Egress bandwidth | No charge | No egress fees. |
| API operations | Included | No per-request charges. |

Pricing is usage-based. You pay for what you store, measured as the average storage consumed across the billing period. There are no minimum commitments.

## Payment

Payments are processed through Stripe. You can add a credit card from **Billing** in the left sidebar of the dashboard.

- Invoices are generated monthly.
- Usage details are visible in **Billing** in the left sidebar.
- Payment is due on receipt of invoice.

## Payment failures

If a payment fails, your account enters **past due** status and Stripe will automatically retry the charge. During this period, your data remains accessible and uploads continue to work.

If all retries are exhausted and the subscription is canceled by Stripe, your account transitions to the paid grace period (see below). You will receive email notifications at each stage.

## Cancellation

You can cancel your paid plan at any time from the dashboard. After cancellation, your account enters a **30-day grace period**:

| Phase | Duration | What you can do |
|-------|----------|-----------------|
| **Grace period** | 30 days | **Read-only.** Download your data or reactivate your plan. No new uploads. |
| **Expired** | After grace period | Account closed. All data permanently deleted. API keys disabled. |

Reactivating your plan during the grace period restores full access immediately.
