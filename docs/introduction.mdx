## Why Fil One

**S3-compatible from day one.** Use the tools you already know — AWS CLI, boto3, any S3-compatible SDK. Point your existing code at our endpoint and it works.

**Object Lock (WORM compliance) on every bucket, by default.** Every bucket has Object Lock enabled automatically — you cannot create a bucket without it. Set a retention policy and Compliance mode ensures no one — not even account admins — can delete or overwrite protected objects until retention expires. This is what makes Fil One suitable for SEC 17a-4, FINRA, HIPAA, and GDPR compliance use cases.

**Verifiable data integrity.** Data stored on Fil One is backed by Filecoin, a distributed storage network where storage providers must continuously prove they are holding your data through cryptographic proofs. Your data's integrity is independently verifiable, not just claimed.

**No egress fees.** Download your data as often as you need. There are no data transfer charges.

**Simple, flat pricing.** $4.99 / TB / month. No per-request charges, no hidden fees, no tiered pricing.

**30-day free trial.** 1 TB of storage and 2 TB of bandwidth, no credit card required.
